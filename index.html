About Me

Welcome to my website, my name is Faheem! I'm a woodworker in the metro Atlanta area. I started woodworking in 2023 because I wanted to make custom picture frames for myself. Since then, I expanded to cutting boards, charcuterie boards, boxes, tabletops, and coffee tables. I now have the pleasure to work with extremely rare and one-of-a-kind pieces of wood.

I source the slabs for my larger projects from the Pacific Northwest. The PNW gets a lot of rain, which, combined with the temperate climate, allows for long growing seasons and for the trees to reach immense sizes over centuries. This contributes to large, wide slabs of wood that are the most sought after on the planet. The color is unlike anything else you will ever see, and that's a promise. Don't believe me? See for yourself in my Commission Ready Slabs section and decide for yourself.
